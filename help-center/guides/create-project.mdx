---

## Overview

Projects help organize documentation for each of your products or teams.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Click "Projects" in the sidebar of your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Project">
    Click "Create Project" and enter a name, e.g. "Website Redesign".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages">
    Use "New Page" to start adding documentation to your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Team">
    Add team members to collaborate on the project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
