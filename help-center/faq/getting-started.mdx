title: How do I get started with Converted?
description: Learn the first steps for new users.
---

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://converted.com`.
2. Create your first workspace from the dashboard.
3. Add team members via the "Team" tab.
4. Click "New Page" to start your first document.

## Tips

- Use markdown to format your docs.
- Organize pages by project.
- Invite teammates anytime from settings.
