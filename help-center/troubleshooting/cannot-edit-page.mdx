---
title: Cannot Edit a Page
description: Quick fix for editing issues in Converted.
---

## Symptoms

- "Edit" button is disabled
- Page shows as read-only

## Causes

- No edit permissions
- Page is locked by another user

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Ensure you're an editor or admin in the workspace settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock Page">
    If the page is locked, wait or contact the person editing it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh">
    Refresh your browser or log out and back in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
